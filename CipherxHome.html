CipheX is a online programming club that is intended for programmers only. The online club is free for everyone as long as you are passionate about programming. We as the CipheX club we provide usable resources related to web designing and java such as books and videos that might help you improve your programming skills.


The CipheX programming club first started with a small group of people coming together to create small projects and learn about programming. The CipheX programming club is located in South Africa(Free State) but tutorials.


The CipheX programming club invites every programmer from beginner to advanced to join so that we can take the computing feild to great heights. You can also upload projects whether they are completed or not because we are willing to work together on projects.
